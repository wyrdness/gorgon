# Sources & References

## Ancient Sources

### Primary Greek & Roman Texts
- Hesiod. *Theogony*, circa 700 BCE. (Lines 270-283: Birth and description of Gorgons)
- Homer. *Iliad*, circa 8th century BCE. (References to Gorgon imagery)
- Homer. *Odyssey*, circa 8th century BCE. (Book 11: Gorgon in Hades)
- Pindar. *Pythian Ode 12*, 5th century BCE. (Athena and the Gorgon)
- Aeschylus. *Prometheus Bound*, 5th century BCE. (Gorgons as guardians)
- Apollodorus. *Bibliotheca*, 1st-2nd century CE. (Perseus myth)
- Ovid. *Metamorphoses*, 8 CE. (Book 4: Medusa's transformation story)
- Pausanias. *Description of Greece*, 2nd century CE. (Gorgon imagery in temples)

### Ancient Art & Archaeology
- Temple of Artemis pediment, Corfu (circa 580 BCE)
- Black-figure and red-figure pottery (7th-4th century BCE)
- Gorgoneion shield devices and architectural ornaments
- Roman mosaics and frescoes
